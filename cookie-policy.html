Overview

GUARDER is an independent home security comparison platform. This website may use cookies, pixels, tags, local storage, and similar technologies to help the site work correctly, remember preferences, measure activity, and improve the visitor experience.

Cookies do not make GUARDER a direct installer, contractor, alarm monitoring company, or emergency response provider. GUARDER helps visitors compare security service categories and request provider options.

Important note

By continuing to use this website, you may allow certain cookies depending on your browser settings and cookie choices. You can control cookies through your browser or device settings.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They can help a website recognize a browser, remember settings, support security, understand page activity, and improve performance.

Similar technologies may include pixels, tags, scripts, local storage, and analytics identifiers. In this policy, the word “cookies” includes these similar technologies where appropriate.

Types of Cookies We May Use

Essential cookies

Essential cookies help the website function properly. They may support page navigation, form behavior, cookie banner preferences, security, and basic technical operations.

Preference cookies

Preference cookies may remember choices such as cookie banner status, interface preferences, or other settings that improve the browsing experience.

Analytics cookies

Analytics cookies may help us understand how visitors interact with the website, which pages are viewed, and where the user experience can be improved.

Marketing or measurement cookies

Marketing or measurement cookies may help evaluate website performance, referral sources, form interactions, or the effectiveness of campaigns. These cookies may be set by GUARDER or by third-party tools.

Why We Use Cookies

Cookies may be used to support reliable website operation and improve how visitors interact with GUARDER.

  • To keep the website working correctly across pages and devices.
  • To remember cookie preferences or similar user choices.
  • To measure page visits, clicks, form interactions, and general usage trends.
  • To improve website speed, layout, content, and usability.
  • To help protect the website from misuse, errors, or technical issues.
  • To understand which security categories visitors are interested in comparing.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be placed by third-party services that support website hosting, analytics, form processing, advertising measurement, security, or other platform functions.

Third-party services may collect information according to their own policies. GUARDER does not control every cookie or tracking technology used by third-party providers once it is set by those services.

  • Analytics tools may collect information about site usage and page performance.
  • Security tools may help detect misuse, spam, or technical problems.
  • Advertising or measurement tools may help understand referral sources or campaign activity.
  • Embedded services may set cookies if used on certain pages.

Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block cookies, delete existing cookies, receive alerts when cookies are used, or limit certain types of tracking.

  • You can delete cookies already stored on your device.
  • You can block all cookies or only third-party cookies.
  • You can configure browser privacy controls.
  • You can use device settings or browser extensions to limit tracking.

If you block or delete cookies, some parts of the website may not function as expected. For example, forms, preferences, cookie banner status, or analytics opt-out choices may not work correctly.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

GUARDER may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in website functionality, technology, legal requirements, or business practices.

When changes are made, the “Last updated” date may be revised. Continued use of the website after updates means the revised Cookie Policy applies.
