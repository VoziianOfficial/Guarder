Aggregator model

GUARDER organizes provider options before you choose.

Home security decisions can involve equipment packages, monitoring plans, contracts, app access, installation methods, and local availability. GUARDER makes those comparison points easier to review.

What GUARDER does

GUARDER helps homeowners compare service categories, understand provider-related factors, and request quotes from independent provider options.

What GUARDER does not do

GUARDER does not provide installation, monitoring, repair, emergency response, or contractor services directly.

What homeowners control

Homeowners can review available categories, compare provider factors, ask questions, and choose which provider options to contact.
