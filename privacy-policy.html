Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how GUARDER may collect, use, and protect information when visitors use this independent home security comparison platform.

Overview

GUARDER is an independent home security comparison platform. We help visitors review and request provider options for categories such as alarm systems, security cameras, smart locks, home monitoring, access control, and video doorbells.

GUARDER does not directly install security systems, provide monitoring services, dispatch emergency services, or perform contractor work. Independent providers are responsible for their own services, pricing, licenses, insurance, terms, and customer communications.

Important note

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through this website. If you interact with an independent provider, that provider may have its own privacy practices and policies.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly when you complete a contact form, request provider options, call us, email us, or interact with website features.

Information you may provide

  • Name and contact details, such as phone number and email address.
  • ZIP code, city, or service area information.
  • Home security category of interest, such as alarm systems or cameras.
  • Message details you choose to submit through a form.
  • Consent confirmations related to provider option requests.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, such as browser type, device information, pages viewed, referring pages, approximate location information, and interaction data.

How We Use Information

We may use collected information to operate the website, respond to inquiries, help visitors request provider options, improve website performance, and maintain platform security.

  • To process and respond to contact or quote request forms.
  • To help organize provider option requests by service category and location.
  • To communicate with visitors about their submitted request.
  • To improve website content, usability, performance, and security.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations or enforce website terms.

Information Sharing

We may share information with service providers, technology vendors, business partners, or independent provider options when necessary to operate the platform or respond to a request.

  • Independent provider options related to your selected security category or service area.
  • Website hosting, analytics, form processing, customer support, or security vendors.
  • Legal, compliance, or safety-related recipients when required or appropriate.

We do not guarantee that a particular provider will be available in every area. Provider availability, pricing, terms, licenses, insurance, and services may vary.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This website may use cookies and similar technologies to support basic site functionality, remember preferences, understand website usage, and improve visitor experience.

  • Essential cookies help the website function correctly.
  • Preference cookies may remember settings or choices.
  • Analytics cookies may help us understand page visits and interactions.
  • Marketing or measurement cookies may help evaluate website performance.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Some website features may not work correctly if cookies are disabled.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a form or provide certain information. You may also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information where applicable.

  • You can avoid submitting optional message details.
  • You can unsubscribe or ask us not to contact you where applicable.
  • You can adjust browser cookie controls.
  • You can contact us about privacy-related questions.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. However, no website, data transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Visitors should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information through general website forms unless specifically requested and appropriate.
